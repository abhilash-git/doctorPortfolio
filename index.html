About Me

Dr. Jerin James D'silva is a dedicated and compassionate pulmonologist with over 15 years of experience in the field. Holding an MD in Pulmonology, Dr. D'silva has specialized in emergency medicine, providing critical care and life-saving treatments in high-pressure environments. His commitment to patient care and his innovative approach to treatment have earned him recognition and respect in the medical community. Dr. D'silva is passionate about advancing medical knowledge and improving patient outcomes through research and continuous learning.
